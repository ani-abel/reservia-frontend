PRIVACY POLICY

The type of personal data we collect

RESERVIA is an app for managing your files locally. To be able to achieve this goal in the best way possible, we need certain account permissions from our users. These include the following:

  • Access to profile data
    This allows us to know who you are and gives us access to your basic profile data from either DROPBOX or GOOGLE
  • Access to files
    This allows us to display your files and allow you to manage those files through our platform. It also allows you to manage your file's metadata through RESERVIA
  • Downloading of files
    This permission allows you to download your files this app

NOTE: If you choose to reject any of the above stated permissions, you will NOT be able to use this app. RESERVIA does not store your personal data.
